Treatment Algorithm

Type 1

Diagnosis of Type 1 Diabetes
↓
Initiate Basal + Bolus Insulin
↓
Consider CGM / Insulin Pump
↓
A1c Goal < 7% (Individualize)

Type 2 (ADA-Based)

Start Lifestyle + metformin (if not contraindicated)
↓
Assess Comorbidities
ASCVD
GLP-1 RA OR SGLT2 inhibitor (CV benefit)
Heart Failure
SGLT2 inhibitor preferred
CKD
SGLT2 inhibitor if eGFR adequate
Weight Priority
GLP-1 RA preferred
↓
If A1c ≥ 10% OR symptomatic → Initiate Insulin
